Library Services

Book Lending Service / Circulation

Library provides open access for all books to its Students, Faculty, and Staff.

Book Reservation

Books can be reserved at the circulation desk or through the intranet if they are not available on the shelf.

Reference Service

In-person assistance is provided by the library to help users fulfil their information needs.

User Guidance Service

A Library Orientation Programme is conducted to introduce new members to the library's resources.

Databases Training Support

Workshops and training programs are offered to promote self-reliant learning and database proficiency.

Reprographic Service

Photocopying is available through the reprographic centre at 1 per page.
